Frequently Asked Questions

Who can participate?

The event is open to students from all colleges. You just need a valid college ID card to enter the campus. We also have categories for school students in specific events.

Is there a registration fee?

Entry to the campus and expo area is free. However, specific workshops and the hackathon have a nominal registration fee as mentioned in the passes section.

Will accommodation be provided?

Yes, accommodation is available for out-of-station participants on a first-come, first-serve basis in the college hostels. Charges apply for non-VIP pass holders.

What should I bring?

Bring your college ID (mandatory), laptop (for workshops/hackathon), chargers, and a water bottle. If you're staying overnight, bring basic toiletries.
